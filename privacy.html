Privacy Notice

This page describes the minimum privacy behavior of the current Anestha prototype as shared for colleague review and workflow feedback.

What the app stores locally

Anestha stores some preferences in your browser so the app can restore your working state between visits.

  • selected language
  • favorite and recent drugs
  • custom drug presets
  • saved template setups
  • other calculator state needed for local restore

This local data is stored in your browser through local storage. In the current prototype, it is not synced to a cloud account.

Feedback form data

If you use the feedback links inside the Support tab, your response is submitted through Google Forms.

Feedback responses may include information you choose to enter, such as comments, screenshots, reference links, and an optional email address.

Do not submit patient identifiers or other sensitive personal health information through the feedback form.

External links

The app contains links to external medical references and support channels. Those destinations have their own privacy and data practices.

Examples include DailyMed, PubMed, OpenAnesthesia, Google Forms, and any future support links such as Toss or Ko-fi.

Contact and updates

This privacy notice is a practical prototype-level notice and should be expanded if the app is shared more broadly, adds analytics, or introduces login and cloud sync.
